FAQ

Can I use EduAble to record my lectures?
Please ensure that recording lecture content is allowed by your institution's policies. EduAble is not responsible for any misuse of its services.
I have a disability that makes it difficult for me to take notes. Can I use EduAble?
Absolutely! EduAble was designed specifically with accessibility in mind. At the moment, you can speak to take notes, rather than having to use a keyboard or pen and paper to write them out. We'd love to hear your thoughts on how we can make EduAble even more accessible, so please drop us a line and we'd love to add your ideas to our roadmap.
